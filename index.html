Chongyun

Chongyun is a young exorcist from Liyue who is endowed with a positive disposition. He is a master of exorcism and uses his Cryo abilities to defeat evil spirits.

Abilities

Chongyun wields a claymore and has Cryo abilities that allow him to freeze enemies and deal massive damage.

Background

Chongyun comes from a family of exorcists and has trained from a young age to master his abilities and protect the people of Liyue.

Personality

Chongyun is known for his calm and composed demeanor. Despite his young age, he is highly skilled and dedicated to his craft.

Training

Chongyun has undergone rigorous training since childhood, honing his skills in exorcism and mastering his Cryo abilities.
